Our Services

K2A Management and Save Earth Save Life Joint Venture provides a comprehensive range of environmental services designed to promote sustainable development in Cambodia. Our key services include:

Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA)

Conducting thorough assessments to identify, analyze, and mitigate the environmental impacts of development projects, ensuring compliance with national and international regulations.

Biodiversity Surveys


Performing detailed studies of local flora and fauna to understand and preserve the region's biodiversity, with a focus on maintaining or enhancing protected areas and ecosystems.

Ecology Surveys



Performing thorough ecological research to examine interactions within ecosystems, monitor their health, and propose efficient management approaches.

Renewable Energy Projects

Developing and implementing renewable energy solutions, such as solar, wind, and hydroelectric power, to reduce carbon emissions and promote cleaner energy alternatives.

Key Strength

Expertise and Experience

Combining decades of experience from both organizations, the joint venture is equipped with the knowledge and skills needed to tackle complex environmental issues.
